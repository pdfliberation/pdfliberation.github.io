The PDF Liberation Hackathon took place in six cities between January 17 and 19. In Washington DC, New York and San Francisco we had judging.  Following were our prize winners:

#### Washington, DC
1. [What Word Where](https://github.com/pdfliberation/whatwordwhere)
2. [US AID Development Experience Clearinghouse](https://github.com/pdfliberation/USAID-DEC)

#### New York
1. [Jersey City Budget PDF Liberation](https://github.com/pdfliberation/Jersey-City-Budget-PDF-Liberation)
2. [New York Economic Development Commission Newsletter](https://github.com/pdfliberation/NYCEDCprosedatascraper)

#### San Francisco
1. [Amnesty International Data](https://github.com/pdfliberation/amnestydata)
2. [House Financial Disclosures](https://github.com/pdfliberation/housedisc)
